BALAYAN

Balayan, Batangas, is a historic town known for its rich cultural heritage, scenic landscapes, and vibrant festivals. Once a key Spanish settlement, it retains historical landmarks like the Immaculate Conception Parish. The town is famous for its *Parada ng Lechon* festival on June 24, where roasted pigs are paraded to honor St. John the Baptist. Balayan's mix of cultural charm and natural beauty, including beaches and farmland, makes it a notable destination in Batangas.
